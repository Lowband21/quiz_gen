Question: In an in-order traversal of a binary tree, what is the correct order of operations?
Possible Answers: 
a) Visit the root node, process the left child, then process the right child
b) Process the left child, visit the root node, then process the right child
c) Process the right child, visit the root node, then process the left child
d) Visit the root node, process the right child, then process the left child
Key: b
