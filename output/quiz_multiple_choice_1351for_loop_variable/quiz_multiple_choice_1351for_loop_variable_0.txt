Question: In Python, what does the expression "range(n)" produce?

Possible Answers:
a) A sequence of n integers starting from 1 and ending at n.
b) A sequence of n integers starting from 0 and ending at n.
c) A sequence of n integers starting from 1 and ending at n+1.
d) A sequence of n integers starting from 0 and ending at n-1.

Key: d
