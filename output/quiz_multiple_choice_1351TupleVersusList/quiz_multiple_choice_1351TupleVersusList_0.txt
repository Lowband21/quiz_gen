Question: What is a key difference between tuples and lists in Python, and when might you prefer to use one over the other?
Possible Answers: 
a) Tuples and lists are identical, there is no difference in their usage.
b) Tuples are mutable and lists are immutable, you would use a tuple when you need your data to change.
c) Tuples are immutable and lists are mutable, you would use a tuple when you need your data to be unchangeable.
